# WAL Protocol
1. **Update Frequency**: Update `WAL.md` at the end of every session, before any destructive operation, or when switching contexts.
2. **Size constraint**: Keep `WAL.md` under 3000 tokens. Collapse completed items into single lines.
3. **Structure**: Must contain `Current Phase`, `Completed`, `In Progress`, `Known Issues`, `Decisions Pending`, and `Session Context`.
